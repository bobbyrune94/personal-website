    University Wushu Games

    The University Wushu Games (UWG) is an annual competition held at the University of Maryland College Park at the end of the Fall semester hosted by their Wushu club. Practitioners from around the DMV (DC-Maryland-Virginia) area and those from colleges along the East Coast come and compete in different barehand and weapon forms. Our club has made it an annual tradition to go and compete and it's often the first competition that many of our beginners attend.
